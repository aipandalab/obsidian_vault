## 🎯 Part 20:

- Pull yourself together. 振作起来，打起精神
---
> You need to pull yourself together and finish the project. 你得振作起来，把这个项目完成
> Pull yourself together. It's not the end of the world. 打起精神来，这又不是世界末日。

- You read my mind. 你懂我呀 / 你说到我心里去了
---
> Let's order pizza tonight. -- You read my mind! 今晚吃披萨吧。-- 你懂我啊！
> I was about to say the same thing. You totally read my mind. 我正要说同样的话，你完全说到我心坎上。

- Not a chance. 想都别想
---
> Can I borrow your car tonight? -- Not a chance. 我今晚可以借你的车吗？ -- 想都别想。
> He asked if I would go back to my ex. Not a chance! 他问我会不会回头找前任，不可能的！

- Don't push your luck. 别得寸进尺
--- 
> I already helped you once. Don't push your luck. 我已经帮你一次了，别得寸进尺啊
> You were late twice this week. Don't push your luck. 你这周已经迟到两次了，别再挑战底线

- Stop beating around the bush. 别再拐弯抹角啦
--- 
> Stop beating around the bush and tell me the truth. 别拐弯抹角了，直接告诉我真相
> You're beating around the bush. What exactly do you want? 你在拐弯抹角，你到底想说什么？

- I'm not taking the fall for this. 这锅我不背
---
> If something goes wrong, I'm not taking the fall for this. 要是出问题，我可不背锅。
> Don't drag me into it. I'm not taking the fall for your mistake. 别把我扯进去，你的错我不背锅

- You are missing the point. 你没搞清楚 / 你理解错了
---
> You're missing the point. I'm not blaming you. 你误会了，我不是在怪你。
> You're missing the point. I'm trying to help you. 你没理解重点，我是在帮你

- Don't make a fuss. 别大惊小怪的
--- 
> It's just a small scratch. Don't make a fuss. 只是个小刮伤，别大惊小怪的
> Don't make a fuss over nothing. 别为一点小事大惊小怪的

- I'm running on fumes. 我已经精疲力尽了(快要燃烧殆尽的感觉)
---
> I slept only three hours last night. I'm running on fumes today. 我昨晚只睡了三小时，今天整个快没电了
> After working all weekend, I'm running on fumes. 周末都在加班，我已经精疲力尽了

- You asked for it. 这是你自找的 / 你这是咎由自取
---
> You didn't listen to my warning. You asked for it. 我都提醒你了，这是你自找的。
> If you stay up all night, you'll be sleepy tomorrow. You asked for it. 你要是通宵的话，明天困就是你自找的
