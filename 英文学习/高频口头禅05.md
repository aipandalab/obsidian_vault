## 🎯 Part 5:

- What's going on? 发生什么事了？
---
> Hey, what's going on? You look upset. 嘿，怎么了？你看起来不太开心
> I just got here. What's going on? 我刚到，发生什么事情了？

- Long time no see. 好久不见
---
> Long time no see! How have you been? 好久不见！最近过得怎么样？
> Wow, long time no see. You haven't change a bit! 哇，好久不见！你一点都没变！

- Take your time. 不着急，慢慢来
---
> No rush, take your time. 别急，慢慢来
> Take you time with the report, accuracy matters more. 那份报告慢慢做，准确度更重要。

- It's up to you. 你来决定
---
> Do we eat out or stay home? It's up to you. 我们出去吃还是在家吃？ 你来决定吧
> Whether we go or not, it's up to you. 要不要去，全看你

- You never know. 谁知道呢
---
> 	Maybe she'll change her mind. You never know. 也许她会改变主意，谁知道呢
> 	You might win the lottery someday, you never know!  说不定哪天你会中彩票呢！

- I mean it. 我是认真的。
---
> I'm not joking this time. I mean it. 这次我不是开玩笑的，我是认真的
> You really should take a break, I mean it. 你真的该休息一下，我是认真的。

- You made my day. 你让我今天超开心
---
> Thanks for the surprise gift, you made my day! 谢谢你的惊喜礼物，你让我今天好开心
> Your message really made my day. 你的讯息真的让我一整天都很开心

- That's a good one. 这句话真妙
---
> Haha! That's a good one, you always make me laugh. 哈哈！这句话真妙，你总能逗我笑。
> Oh, that's a good one, I didn't expect that joke!  喔，这句真好笑，我完全没想到

- Don't get me wrong. 别误会我的意思。
---
> Don't get me wrong, I like your idea, but it needs work. 别误会，我喜欢你的想法，但还需要改进
> Don't get me wrong, I'm not mad, just surprised. 别误会，我不是生气，只是有点惊讶

- I'm on my way. 我在路上了
---
> Don't worry, I'm on my way! 别担心，我在路上了！
> Traffic's bad, but I'm on my way now. 交通有点堵，但我已经在路上了。
