## 🎯 Part 4:

- Why didn't I think of that? 为什么我没想到呢？
---
> That's such a great idea. Why didn't I think of that? 那真是个好主意，为什么我没想到呢？
> Wow, smart move! Why didn't I think of that before? 哇，聪明的做法！我之前怎么没想到呢？

- That's not the case.  情况不是那样的
---
> Some people think I'm rich, but that's not the case. 有人以为我很有钱，但情况不是那样的
> He said I was angry, but that's not the case. 他说我很生气，但不是那样的

- You can say that again. 你说的太对了！(完全同意)
---
> This weather is terrible. You can say that again! 天气太糟了，你说的太对了！
> That movies was amazing. answer：You can say that again! 那部电影太棒了。答：完全同意

- Let's call it a day. 今天到此为止吧
---
> We've done enough work. Let's call it a day. 我们已经做的够多了，今天就到此为止吧
> It's getting late, Let's call it a day. 时间不早了，今天就收工吧。

- Better late than never. 迟到总比不到好
---
> I finally finished the book. Better late than never!  我终于把这本书看完了。迟到总比不到好！
> He came to apologize. Well, better late than never. 他来道歉了。嗯，迟到总比不到好。

- It's not worth it. 这不值得。
---
> Don't argue with him, It's not worth it.  别和他争论了，不值得。
> Spending so much money on that bag? It's not worth it. 花那么多钱买那个包？太不值得了。

- Easier said than done.  说起来容易做起来难
---
> Quitting smoking? That's easier said than done. 戒烟？说起来容易做起来难。
> Moving abroad sounds great, but that's easier said than done. 移居海外听起来不错，但说起来容易做起来难

- Don't take it personally.  别往心里去。
---
> He was rude, but don't take it personally. 他有点无礼，但别往心里去
> I just gave you some feedback, don't take it personally. 我只是给你一些反馈，别放在心上。

- I'm keeping my fingers crossed. 我在祈求好运
---
> I'm keeping my fingers crossed for your exam tomorrow. 祝你明天考试好运
> The weather looks bad, but I'm keeping my fingers crossed. 天气看起来不妙，但我祈求好结果

- That's the last straw. 我受够了！(压垮骆驼的最后一根稻草)
---
> He broke my phone. That's the last straw!  他弄坏了我的手机，我受够了
> She lied again. That's the last straw. 她又撒谎了，我真的受够了。
