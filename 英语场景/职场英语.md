# 🎯 职场闲聊
## 打招呼
- How's your day so far? 今天过得怎么样？
- How's your morning so far?早上过的怎么样？
- How‘s your week so far? 这周过得怎么样啊？
## 问名字
- 问：I haven't caught your name. 我还没有听过你的名字耶
- 答：Felix, and you?
## 加深聊天
- Got any fun plans this week? 这周有没有安排什么好玩的事啊？
- How's your family doing? 你家人最近都还好吗？
- I recently watched/went to/found out ... 我最近看了/去了/发现...
- I recently found out that once you turn 30, you lose about 6-8% of muscle mass every year. So I just signed up a Crossfit gym close to my house. 我最近发现一旦过了30岁，每年大概会流失6-8%的肌肉量，所以我刚报名了我家附近的一间Crossfit健身房
- I'll let you get back to work. 那你去忙吧！
# 🎯 会议英文
- Good to see you all. 很开心看到大家
- We will go over ... 我们要来讨论...
- We will go over where out leads came from for this past month. 我们来讨论一下这个月的潜在客户名单事哪里来的
- I'd like to add something/build on that idea. 我想补充一点/在这个想法上延伸一下
- Just to make sure I understand. 为了确认我有理解你的意思
- That's all from me. 我要说的就这些
# 🎯 职场沟通
- I see you point and it will help reduce duplicate work hours if we can all log our work hours every day. 我懂你的意思，如果大家每天记录工作内容确实可以减少做重复事情的状况
- Have we considered ... 我们有没有考虑过...
- Could you clarify that for me? 能不能多说明一下？
- Can you elaborate on that? 意思同上
- I'm working on it, and it should be done by Friday. 我正在做，预计周五可以完成
