

### ☕ 场景一：Catching up at the cafe (在咖啡馆叙旧)

**A:** **Hello, it is nice to see you.**

**甲：** 你好，很高兴见到你。

**B:** **Hello, it has been a long time.**

**乙：** 你好，好久不见了。

**A:** **Yes, many months have passed. How are you doing these days?**

**甲：** 是的，好几个月过去了。你最近过得怎么样？

**B:** **I am doing very well today.**

**乙：** 我今天过得非常好。

**A:** **I am glad to hear that. Are you still working in the city?**

**甲：** 听到你这么说我很高兴。你还在城里工作吗？

**B:** **Yes, I still work at the bank.**

**乙：** 是的，我还在银行工作。

**A:** **Do you like your job down there?**

**甲：** 你喜欢在那里的工作吗？

**B:** **Yes, I like my job very much. The cafe is very busy right now.**

**乙：** 是的，我非常喜欢我的工作。咖啡馆现在很忙。

**A:** **Yes, many people are drinking coffee. Have you ordered your hot drink yet?**

**甲：** 是的，很多人在喝咖啡。你点热饮了吗？

**B:** **No, I was waiting for you first.**

**乙：** 还没有，我先等你。

**A:** **That is very kind of you. What would you like to drink today?**

**甲：** 你太好了。你今天想喝点什么？

**B:** **I would like a large hot coffee.**

**乙：** 我想要一大杯热咖啡。

**A:** **Do you want milk with it?**

**甲：** 里面要加牛奶吗？

**B:** **Yes, a little milk and no sugar.**

**乙：** 是的，加一点牛奶，不要加糖。

**A:** **I will order the drinks for us.**

**甲：** 我去帮我们点喝的。

**B:** **Thank you, I will pay you back.**

**乙：** 谢谢，我会把钱给你的。

**A:** **No, this hot coffee is a gift. You are always a very good friend.**

**甲：** 不用，这杯热咖啡我请客。你一直都是个很好的朋友。

**B:** **I will go to the counter now. I will save this small round table.**

**乙：** 我现在去柜台（注：此处根据语境应是甲去柜台，乙占座，原文为顺承对话）。我来占这张小圆桌。

**A:** **Thank you, I will be very quick.**

**甲：** 谢谢，我会很快的。

**B:** **Take your time, I am not busy.**

**乙：** 慢慢来，我不忙。

_(片刻后)_

**A:** **I have the hot drinks right here. They smell very fresh and very good.**

**甲：** 热饮拿来了。它们闻起来很新鲜，很香。

**B:** **The coffee is very hot right now. I will wait for it to cool.**

**乙：** 咖啡现在很烫。我等它凉一下。

**A:** **How is your family doing these days?**

**甲：** 你家人最近好吗？

**B:** **My family is doing very well too.**

**乙：** 我家人也都很好。

**A:** **Is your brother still at school?**

**甲：** 你弟弟还在上学吗？

**B:** **Yes, he studies very hard every day.**

**乙：** 是的，他每天学习都很刻苦。

**A:** **That is very good news for him.**

**甲：** 这对他来说是个好消息。

**B:** **Yes, we are very proud of him.**

**乙：** 是的，我们为他感到骄傲。

**A:** **I am very happy for your family.**

**甲：** 我为你的家人感到高兴。

**B:** **Thank you. The coffee is perfect now.**

**乙：** 谢谢。咖啡现在温度刚好。

**A:** **Yes, it is a very good drink. It is very nice to talk again.**

**甲：** 是的，非常好喝。能再次和你聊天真好。

**B:** **We must do this again very soon.**

**乙：** 我们很快必须再聚一次。

**A:** **I completely agree with you on that. Have a very nice day.**

**甲：** 我完全同意你的看法。祝你有美好的一天。

**B:** **You too, enjoy the rest of it.**

**乙：** 你也是，好好享受剩下的时光。

**A:** **Thank you again, see you very soon. Goodbye.**

**甲：** 再次感谢，很快再见。再见。

**B:** **Goodbye.**

**乙：** 再见。
