## 🎯 Part 21:

- Get back on the horse. 重新振作 / 重新开始
---
> After failing the exam, he decided to get back on the horse and study harder.考试失败后，他决定重新振作，更努力地读书
> Life is full of ups and downs; you just have to get back on the horse after a setback.  人生充满起伏，挫折之后你只能重新振作

- I'm not falling for this. 我才不上当呢
---
> He tried to trick me with a fake story, but I'm not falling for this. 他想用假故事骗我，但我才不上当呢
> Don't even try to blame me for that mistake. I'm not falling for this. 别想把那个错怪到我头上，我才不上当呢

- It's none of your business. 不关你的事 / 与你无关
---
> Why are you asking about my salary? It's none of your business. 你为什么问我的薪水？这不关你的事。
> Stop interfering in their argument. It's none of your business. 别再干涉他们的争吵，这与你无关

- You went to far! 你太过分了
---
> Criticizing her appearance in public? You went too far. 公开批评她的外貌？太过分了
> Joking about his family like that? You went too far. 那样开他家人的玩笑？你做得太过头了

- It's not my place. 我无权过问
---
> I know it's not my place, but I think you should talk to him. 我知道我无权过问，但我觉得你应该跟他谈谈。
> I know they're having problems, but it's not my place to get involved. 我知道他们有问题，但我无权参与其中。

- It's now or never. 机不可失，失不再来。
---
> If you want to apply for the scholarship, it's now or never. 如果你想申请这个奖学金，现在不做更待何时。
> He knew he had to speak up in the meeting, it's now or never. 他知道在会议中必须发言，机不可失。

- No hard feelings 别介意 / 别放在心上
---
> I accidentally stepped on his foot. He laughed and said, "No hard feelings" 我不小心踩到他的脚，他笑着说：别介意
> We had a disagreement, but no hard feelings, right? 我们有些分歧，但别放在心上，好吗？

- Knock it off. 少来这套 / 就此打住
---
> Stop teasing your sister, knock it off! 别再逗你妹了，就此打住
> Stop playing tricks, knock it off! 你不要再耍心眼了，少来这套

- Suck it up 忍一忍吧 / 咬牙承受
---
> I know the workout is tough, but you have to suck it up. 我知道健身很累，但你得忍一忍
> She was nervous about the speech, but decided to suck it up and go on stage. 她对演讲很紧张，但还是决定咬紧牙关上台

- Hold your horses. 稍安勿躁 / 别着急
---
> Hold your horses, we haven't even started the meeting yet. 稍安勿躁，我们的会议还没开始呢
> Hold your horses, I'll finish this task in a minute. 别着急，我一会儿就完成这个任务
