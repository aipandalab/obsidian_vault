## 🎯 Part 22:

- Be there or be square. 不见不散 / 一定要来哦(开玩笑语气)
---
> We're having a barbecue this Saturday. Be there of be square! 我们这周六要烤肉，不见不散哦
> I'm throwing a birthday party tonight. Be there or be square. 我今晚办生日派对，你一定要来哦

- Stand up for yourself. 挺直腰杆 / 替自己争取
---
> If you think they're treating you unfairly, you need to stand up for yourself. 如果你觉得他们对你不公平，你要替自己争取
> Don't let people push you around. Stand up for yourself! 别让人欺负你，要挺直腰杆！

- My patience is wearing thin. 我快没耐心了
---
> They've been arguing for an hour, and my patience is wearing thin. 他们吵了一个小时，我快没耐心了
> If you keep ignoring my messages, my patience will wear thin. 你再不回我信息，我就要没耐心了

- Just so you know. 就是给你说一声
---
> Just so you know, I'll be leaving early today. 先跟你说一下，我今天会提早离开
> Just so you know, the meeting got postponed. 跟你说一下，会议延后了

- I'm swamped right now. 我现在忙死了
---
> Can we talk later? I'm swamped right now. 我们能晚点再聊吗？我现在忙死了。
> Sorry for the late reply, I was swamped with work. 抱歉回晚了，我刚被工作淹没

- You don't get a say. 你没有发言权 / 轮不到你决定
---
> This is a family matter. You don't get a say. 这是我的家事，轮不到你决定
> It's my project, so you don't get a say in this. 这是我的项目，所以在这儿你没有发言权

- Take it from me. 听我一句话 / 相信我说的
---
> Take it from me, you'll regret it if you don't prepare early. 听我一句，如果不早做准备，你会后悔的
> Take it from me, he's not someone you can trust. 相信我，他不是一个值得信任的人

- Cut me some slack. 放我一马吧
---
> I was late only once. Cut me some slack. 我只吃到了一次，放我一马吧
> Oh, come on! Cut me some slack, will you? 哦，拜托！放我一马，好吗？

- I'm blown away. 我惊呆了 / 我被惊艳到了
---
`The mountain view is phenomenal! I'm blown away. 这山景太美了，我被惊艳到了`
`When I heard the news, I was completely blown away. 听到消息的那一刻，我整个人都惊呆了`

- I'm at my limit. 我到极限了 / 我撑不住了
---
`I haven't rested for three days. I'm at my limit. 我已经三天没休息了，我快撑不住了`
`This workload is insane. I'm at my limit right now. 这工作量太夸张，我现在已经到极限了`
