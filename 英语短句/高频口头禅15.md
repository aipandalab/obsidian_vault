## 🎯 Part 15:

- Forget about it. 算了吧 / 别提了
---
> I'm really sorry for breaking your glasses. -- Forget about it. It's no big deal. 我真的很抱歉把你眼镜打破了。-- 算了吧，没什么大不了的。
> I tried to explain, but he wouldn't listen.  --  Forget about it. He's not worth your time. 我试着解释，但他根本不听。-- 算了吧，他不值得你浪费时间。

- I'm not sure about that. 我不太确定耶。
---
> He said he can finish it today. -- I'm not sure about that. 他说今天能做完。 -- 嗯，我不太确定哦
> This restaurant is always empty. -- I'm not sure about that place. 这家餐厅都没人。-- 我对这家店不太有信心。

- Lucky you! 你好幸运哦
---
> I'm going on vacation next week. -- Lucky you! 我下周要去度假。-- 好羡慕哦！
> I got free concert tickets. -- Lucky you! 我拿到免费演唱会票了。 -- 你运气真好！

- Don't even start. 别提了(吐槽、抱怨语气)
---
> He canceled the meeting again? -- Don't even start. 他又取消会议了？ -- 别提了。
> Traffic was terrible today. -- Don't even start. 今天交通超级堵。-- 别提了，我懂

- Count me out. 我就不去了
---
> We're going clubbing tonight. -- Count me out. 我们今晚去夜店。-- 我就算了。
> Let's do karaoke! -- Count me out. 咱们去唱K吧！-- 我就不去了

- Count me in. 算我一个。
---
> We're planning a weekend BBQ at the park. -- Sounds great! Count me in. 我们打算周末去公园烤肉。-- 太棒了！算我一个！
> Anyone wants to join the English study group? --  Count me in. I really need to practice my speaking. 有人想参加英语学习小组吗？ -- 算我一个，我真的需要多练口语。

- Let me see. 我看一下
---
> Are you free later? -- Let me see. 你一会儿有空吗？ -- 我看一下
> Can you help me tomorrow? -- Let me see. 你明天可以帮我吗？ -- 我看一下哦

- Not even close. 差远了
---
> Did you finish the job already? -- Not even close. 你已经干完活了？-- 差远了
> Are you done there? -- Not even close. 你弄完了吗？-- 还没呢

- There's no way. 不可能吧
---
> He finished that in one hour? There's no way! 他一小时就完成了？ 不可能！
> She's 50 years old? No way! 她50岁了？ 不可能吧！

- I can't complain. 还行吧
---
> How's everything at work? -- I can't complain. 最近工作怎么样？ -- 还行吧
> How's life recently? -- Can't complain. 最近过得怎么样？ -- 还行吧
