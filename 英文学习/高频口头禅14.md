## 🎯 Part 14:

- You tell me. 你说啊 / 你来告诉我。(反问、无奈的语气)
---
> Why did he suddenly disappear?  -- You tell me. 为什么他突然消失了？-- 我怎么知道啊？
> You tell me why the Wi-Fi only breaks when I have a meeting! 你告诉我为什么一开会Wi-Fi就坏呢？

- Don't push it. 别得寸进尺 / 别太过分了
---
> I already helped you once, don't push it. 我已经帮你一次了，别得寸进尺啊！
> Stop complaining. Don't push is. 别在发牢骚啦，别太过分啊！

- I'm not gonna lie. 老实说 / 坦白讲
---
> I'm not gonna lie, that movie was so boring. 老实说，那部电影很无聊。
> I'm not gonna lie, I really need this coffee. 老实说，我真的需要这杯咖啡。

- I can relate to it.  我懂 / 我感同身受
---
> Parenting is exhausting.  -- I can relate to it.  当父母真的很累。-- 完全懂
> You feel stressed with work? I can relate to it. 你感到工作压力大？我懂你啊

- I owe you one. 我欠你个人情
---
> Thanks for helping me move. I owe you one. 感谢你帮我搬家，我欠你个人情。
> You covered my shift? I owe you one. 你帮我代班了？我欠你一个人情。

- Don't jinx it. 别乌鸦嘴
---
> We haven't had traffic today. Don't jinx it! 今天都没有塞车。 别乌鸦嘴！
> Don't say we'll win for sure. Don't jinx it. 别说我们一定会赢，别乌鸦嘴。

- Any thoughts about it? 有什么想法吗？
---
> We might change the plan. Any thoughts about it? 我们可能改计划，你有什么想法吗？
> Any thoughts about the presentation draft? 对于这份演讲稿你有什么想法？

- To be honest. 老实说 / 坦白讲
---
> To be honest, I don't like this idea. 老实说，我不太喜欢这个想法。
> To be honest, I'm pretty worn out. 说实话，我累死了。

- I doubt it. 我觉得不太可能 / 我不信
---
> He said he'll be on time. I highly doubt it. 他说他会准时？我才不信。
> I doubt it's going to stop raining soon. 喔不觉得雨很快会停

- I'll think about it. 我会考虑一下 / 我想想看
---
> Do you want to join our road trip this weekend? -- Hmm... I'll think about it. 这周末想跟我们一起自驾旅行吗？ -- 嗯...我想想看。
> You should take that job offer, It's a great opportunity. -- I know. I'll think about it carefully. 你应该接受那个工作，这是个好机会。-- 我知道。我会好好考虑的。
