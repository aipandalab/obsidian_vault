## 🎯 Part 23:

- I'm speechless. 我不知道该说什么了 / 我无语了
---
> When he told me the price, I was completely speechless. 他跟我说那个价格时，我完全无语了
> I don't even know how to respond. I'm just speechless. 我不知道该如何回复，完全无语

- I'm so torn. 我很纠结呀
---
> I'm torn about whether or not to switch jobs. 我在纠结要不要换工作
> I'm torn between choosing Iphone and Samsung. 我在纠结于选苹果还是三星

- Give it a rest. 别再讲了 / 歇一歇吧
---
> Give it a rest, I just finished three meetings. 别再讲了，我刚开完三场会议唉
> Can you give it a rest? I need a break. 能不能不讲了？我要歇会儿

- It slipped my mind. 我忘了 / 我没想起来
---
> Sorry, I didn't call you back, it completely slipped my mind. 抱歉没回你电话，我完全忘了
> I was supposed to send the email, but it slipped my mind. 我原本要发出那封邮件的，但我完全忘了

- You're full of it. 你在瞎扯 / 你胡说八道
---
> Oh please, you're full of it. Why don't you tell the truth? 拜托，你在瞎扯！为什么不说实话？
> Don't listen to him. He's full of it. 别理他，他在胡说八道

- Mind your own business! 少管闲事 / 不关你的事
---
> Mind your own business! This has nothing to do with you. 少管闲事！这跟你没关系
> Stop telling me what to do. Mind your own business! 别再指挥我了，少管我

- Some things are better left unsaid. 有些话还是别说的好
---
> I was going to tell her what happened, but some things are better left unsaid. 我原本要告诉她发生了什么，但有些话还是不说好
> Honestly, some things are better left unsaid if you want peace. 老实说，如果你想清静，有些事还是别说比较好。

- The grass is always greener. 这山望着那山高 / 别人的总是好的
---
> You think his job is easy? The grass is always greener. 你以为他的工作轻松？那只是你觉得别人的好
> She keeps thinking other people's lives are perfect. The grass is always greener. 她总是觉得别人的生活精彩，这山望着那山高

- Don't judge a book by its cover. 人不可貌相 / 别以貌取人
---
> He looks quiet, but don't judge a book by its cover. 他看起来很安静，但人不可貌相
> The place looks small, but the food is amazing, don't judge a book by its cover. 这地方很小，但食物超好吃，别被表象迷惑

- I'm tied up 我分身乏术  / 我实在走不开
---
> I'd love to help, but I'm tied up right now. 我很想帮忙，但我现在分身乏术呀
> Sorry, I can't leave my desk, I'm totally tied up. 抱歉，我动不了，实在走不开呀
