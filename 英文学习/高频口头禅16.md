## 🎯 Part 16:

- It could be worse. 本来会更糟呢
---
> It's raining again. -- It could be worse. 又下雨了 -- 还好啦，本来会更惨的
> The line is so long. -- Could be worse. 排队排好久。 -- 至少没更差。

- Let me guess. 让我猜猜。
---
> Let me guess. You forgot your keys again? 我来猜猜。你又忘记带钥匙了？
> Let me guess. You stayed up late playing video games? 我猜到了。你又熬夜打游戏啦？

- I wouldn't say that. 我不这么认为 / 我不会这么说
---
> He's so useless. -- I wouldn't say that. 他好没用啊 -- 我不这么认为
> You're bad at maths. -- I wouldn't say that. 你数学很差呢 -- 我不觉得哦

- I wish I could help. 我也想帮忙(但我帮不上)
---
> Can you drive me to the airport tomorrow morning? -- I wish I could help, but I have to work early. 你明早能载我去机场吗？ 我也想帮忙，但我一早要上班
> I really wish I could help with your project, but I'm swamped this week. 我真的很想帮你做那个项目，但我这周忙得不行

- Consider it done. 抱在我身上。
---
> Can you send me the report by tonight? --  Consider it done. 你今晚能把报告发我吗？-- 包在我身上。
> You need someone to pick up your kid? Consider it done. 你需要人帮你接小孩？交给我吧

- Let me get this straight. 让我捋一下 / 我确认一下
---
> Let me get this straight, you're quitting you job and moving to Paris? 等一下我确认一下，你要辞职搬去巴黎？
> Let me get this straight. You borrowed his car and crashed it? 等等我捋一下，你借了他的车，还撞坏了？

- Take my word for it. 相信我吧 / 听我的准没错
---
> Take my word for it, that restaurant is amazing. 相信我，那家餐厅超棒的
> You'll regret it if you skip this movie, take my word for it. 如果错过这部电影你会后悔的，相信我

- I'm a man of my word. 我说话算话。
---
> Don't worry, I'll finish it by tomorrow. I'm a man of my word. 别担心，我明天之前一定完成。我说话算话。
> He promised to help, and he did, he's a man of his word. 他答应帮忙也真的做到了，他是一个信守承诺的人。

- Whatever you say. 随你怎么说
---
> I'm always right, you know that. -- Whatever you say. 我总是对的，你知道的。-- 随你怎么说吧
> We're going to win for sure! -- Yeah, whatever you say. 我们肯定能赢！-- 好啦，你说什么都对

- If you say so. 你要这么说也行
---
> I think this plan will definitely work! -- Yeah, If you say so. 我觉得这个计划肯定能成！ -- 好吧，你要这么说也行，我保留意见。
> She's the best singer in the world! -- If you say so. 她是全世界最棒的歌手 -- 你要这么说也行
