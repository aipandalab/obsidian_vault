### 🌳 场景二：Making weekend plans

**A:** **Are you completely free this coming weekend?**

**甲：** 这个周末你完全有空吗？

**B:** **Yes, I do not have any plans. Do you want to do something fun?**

**乙：** 是的，我没有任何计划。你想做点有趣的事吗？

**A:** **Yes, that sounds like a good idea. We could go to the new park.**

**甲：** 好的，听起来是个好主意。我们可以去那个新公园。

**B:** **Is the weather going to be nice?**

**乙：** 天气会好吗？

**A:** **Yes, it will be very sunny outside. I love walking in the warm sun.**

**甲：** 是的，外面会很晴朗。我喜欢在温暖的阳光下散步。

**B:** **We can take a small picnic.**

**乙：** 我们可以去进行个小野餐。

**A:** **That is a very good plan. I can bring some fresh fruit for it.**

**甲：** 那是个非常好的计划。我可以带点新鲜水果去。

**B:** **I will bring some nice meat sandwiches. Do you like eating sweet red apples?**

**乙：** 我会带些美味的肉三明治。你喜欢吃甜红苹果吗？

**A:** **Yes, red apples are my favorite fruit.**

**甲：** 是的，红苹果是我最喜欢的水果。

**B:** **I will buy some at the market. What time should we meet up there?**

**乙：** 我会在市场上买一些。我们应该几点在那里见面？

**A:** **Let us meet at 12:00 noon.**

**甲：** 我们中午12点见吧。

**B:** **12:00 is a perfect time. I will wait by the large gate.**

**乙：** 12点是个完美的时间。我会在大门口等。

**A:** **The large front gate of the park?**

**甲：** 公园的正大门吗？

**B:** **Yes, exactly right there by the gate.**

**乙：** 是的，就是大门旁边那里。

**A:** **Will we walk around the big lake?**

**甲：** 我们会绕着大湖散步吗？

**B:** **Yes, the big lake is very beautiful. We can see the small white ducks.**

**乙：** 是的，那个大湖非常漂亮。我们可以看到小白鸭。

**A:** **I can bring some duck food for them.**

**甲：** 我可以给它们带点鸭食。

**B:** **The ducks will be very happy then. Do we need to bring folding chairs?**

**乙：** 鸭子们会很高兴的。我们需要带折叠椅吗？

**A:** **No, we can sit on the grass. I will bring a large soft blanket.**

**甲：** 不用，我们可以坐在草地上。我会带一条大而柔软的毯子。

**B:** **That makes it very comfortable and easy. After the park, we can watch a movie.**

**乙：** 那样会非常舒适和放松。去完公园后，我们可以看场电影。

**A:** **Do you want to go to the cinema?**

**甲：** 你想去电影院吗？

**B:** **We can just watch it at home. That is a very relaxing evening plan.**

**乙：** 我们可以就在家看。那是个非常放松的晚间计划。

**A:** **I will make some hot salty popcorn.**

**甲：** 我会做些热的咸爆米花。

**B:** **You are very good at planning things. I just want to have fun together.**

**乙：** 你很擅长做计划。我只是想我们一起玩得开心。

**A:** **I am very excited for this weekend.**

**甲：** 我对这个周末感到非常兴奋。

**B:** **Me too, it will be very nice.**

**乙：** 我也是，一定会很棒的。

**A:** **I will make the fresh sandwiches tomorrow.**

**甲：** 我明天会做新鲜的三明治。

**B:** **Thank you very much for your help.**

**乙：** 非常感谢你的帮忙。

**A:** **It is my pleasure to make them. I will see you on Saturday then.**

**甲：** 做这些是我的荣幸。那我们周六见。

**B:** **Yes, I will see you on Saturday. Have a very good day at work.**

**乙：** 好的，周六见。祝你工作愉快。

**A:** **You too, enjoy your day. Goodbye.**

**甲：** 你也是，享受你的一天。再见。

**B:** **Goodbye.**

**乙：** 再见。
