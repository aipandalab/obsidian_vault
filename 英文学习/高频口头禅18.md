## 🎯 Part 18:

- We make a day of it. 我们好好玩一天吧
---
> The weather is so great. Let's make a day of it. 天气这么好，我们干脆出去玩一整天吧。
> Since we're already at the beach, let's make a day of it. 既然都到海边了，不如就玩上一整天吧

- What a coincidence! 太巧了吧！
---
> You're here too? What a coincidence! 你也在这里？太巧了吧
> We wore the same jacket today, what a coincidence! 我们今天穿同一件外套耶，太巧了吧

- Is everything good? 一切都还好吗？
---
> You look a bit stressed. Is everything good? 你看起来有点紧张，一切都还好吗？
> I haven't heard from you lately. Is everything good? 最近都没你的消息，一切都还好吗？

- It's a win-win situation. 这是双赢的局面 / 双方都有好处
---
> You help me practice English and I buy you coffee. It's win-win situation. 你帮我练英文，我请你喝咖啡，双赢啊
> We both grow from this collaboration. It's a win-win situation. 合作后双方都能成长，这就是双赢

- It has nothing to do with me. 这跟我没关系
---
> Don't blame me for the delay. It has noting to do with me. 别怪我耽误了，这和我一点关系都没有
> That decision was made by the manager. It has nothing to do with me. 那是经理做的决定，和我一点关系也没有。

- I can't make up my mind. 我拿不定主意
---
> I can't make up my mind about which laptop to buy. 要买哪台笔记本电脑我拿不到主意。
> She still can't make up her mind about moving or staying. 她还拿不定主意到底要搬家还是留下来
补充解释：在英语中，疑问词（如 _what, how, where, which_ 等）后面可以直接接 `to do` 构成一个名词性短语，在句中作宾语。
- `what to do` （做什么）
- `where to go` （去哪里）
- `which to choose` （选哪一个）

- Old habits die hard. 江山易感，本性难移
---
> He still wakes up at 5 a.m. every day, Old habits die hard. 他每天还是五点起床，江山易改，本性难移。
> She keeps checking her phone constantly. Old habits die hard. 她还是忍不住一直看手机，这习惯还改不了啦

- Don't take it seriously. 别太当真
---
> I was just joking, don't take it seriously. 我只是开玩笑的，别太当真。
> He likes to tease people. Don't take it seriously. 他喜欢开别人的玩笑，别太当真

- Listen to your gut feeling. 相信你的直觉
---
> If something feels off, listen to your gut feeling. 如果哪里怪怪的，相信你的直觉
> When making big decisions, always listen to your gut feeling. 做重大决定时，一定要相信你的直觉

- I'll check it out. 我去看看 / 我会试试看
---
> That cafe sounds nice. I'll check it out this weekend. 那家咖啡厅听起来不错，我周末去看看。
> This app is phenomenal. You got to check it out. 这款手机app简直太赞了，你一定要试试看。
