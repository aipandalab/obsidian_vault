## 🎯 Part 8:

- That's how it is. 事情就是这样
---
> Some people just don't change, that's how it is. 有些人就是不会变，事情就是这样。
> Life isn't always fair, that's how it is sometimes. 人生并不总是公平，有时候就是这样

- I couldn't care less. 我一点也不在乎
---
> He can say whatever he wants, I couldn't care less. 他爱说什么就说什么，我才不在乎。
> I couldn't care less about rumors. 我才懒得理哪些谣言。

- I'll pass. 我就算了。
---
> You want to go out tonight? I'll pass, I'm tried. 今晚你想出去吗？ 算了吧，我累了
> Thanks, but I'll pass on dessert. 谢啦，不过甜点我就不吃了

- That's not gonna happen. 那不可能发生
---
> You think I'll quit my job for that? That's not gonna happen. 你觉得我会为那件事辞职？不可能。
> He said He'd pay me back tomorrow, yeah, that's not gonna happen. 他说明天会还钱？才怪呢

- You know what I mean? 你懂我意思吧？
---
> It's not about the money, you know what I mean? 这不是钱的问题，你懂我意思吧？
> He's nice, but a bit too quiet, you know what I mean? 他人很好，但有点太安静了，你懂得吧？

- Tell me about it. 可不是嘛。
---
> This week has been so stressful.  Tell me about it. 这周压力超大。可不是嘛，我也同感。
> My boss keeps changing plans. Tell me about it! 我老板一直改计划。我理解那种痛苦

- Guess what? 你猜怎么着？
---
> Guess what? I got the job! 你猜怎么着？ 我被录取了！
> Guess what? We're going to Paris next month! 你猜猜？我们下个月要去巴黎！


- No way! 不会吧！
---
> I won the lottery!  No way!  我中彩票了！ 不会吧！
> He actually apologized? No way!  他真的道歉了？ 太夸张了吧！

- I'm telling you. 我跟你说。
---
> I'm telling you, this restaurant is amazing. 我跟你说，这家餐厅真的超棒。
> I'm telling you, she's going to win. 我告诉你，她一定会赢。

- Long story short. 长话短说 / 简而言之
---
> Long story short, we missed the flight. 长话短说，我们错过了航班
> Long story short, he ended up moving to Canada. 简单说，他最后搬去加拿大了
