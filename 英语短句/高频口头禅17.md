## 🎯 Part 17:

- I don't have time for this. 我没时间搞这些
---
> I don't have time for this. I'm already late for work. 我没时间搞这些，我上班已经迟到了
> Sorry, I don't have time for this drama today. 抱歉，我今天没空陪你演这些情绪戏

- Don't say that. 别那么说  / 别说这种话
---
> Don't say that. You did your best. 别那么说，你已经很努力了
> Don't say that. You're not a failure. 别说这种话，你才不是失败者

- Would you give me a hand? 能帮我一下吗？
---
> Would you give me a hand with these boxes? 能帮我办一下这些箱子吗？
> Hey, would you give me a hand in the kitchen? 嘿，你能来厨房帮我一下吗？

- Everyone wins. 皆大欢喜 / 双赢
---
> We decided to share the prize money, everyone wins! 我们决定平分奖金，大家都开心啦！
> Let's find a solution where everyone wins. 我们来找个双赢的方案吧

- What do we have here? 这是什么情况？
---
> Well, well... what do we have here? Sneaking snacks again? 喔喔～这是什么情况？又在偷吃零食？
> What do we have here? New shoes? 这是什么？新鞋子吗？

- I wouldn't be surprised. 我一点也不意外 / 我会感到惊讶
---
> If he cancels the meeting again, I wouldn't be surprised. 如果他又取消会议，我一点也不意外
> I wouldn't be surprised if it rains later. 如果一会儿下雨，我也不会惊讶

- It' never gonna happen. 不可能 / 想都别想
---
> You want me to wake up at 5 a.m.? It's never gonna happen. 你想我早上五点起床？不可能啦
> He thinks she'll forgive him again? It's never gonna happen. 他觉得她还会原谅他？根本不可能

- What do you say? 你觉得如何？ / 你怎么说？(用来征求意见)
---
> Pizza tonight? What do you say? 今晚吃披萨？你觉得呢？
> What do you say? Should we give it a try? 你怎么看？我们要不要试试？

- You missed the boat. 你错失良机了
---
> The sale ended yesterday. Sorry, you missed the boat. 折扣昨天就结束了。抱歉，你错过了。
> You should've applied earlier. Now you missed the boat. 你早点申请就好了，现在机会没了。

- What are you into lately? 你最近迷上什么？(问兴趣、嗜好)
---
> Hey, what are you into lately? Any new hobbies? 嘿，你最近迷上什么？有新兴趣吗？
> So, what are you into lately? Still playing guitar? 那你最近在迷什么？还在弹吉他吗？
